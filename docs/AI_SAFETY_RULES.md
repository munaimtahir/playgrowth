# AI Safety Rules

## What the AI may do

- Draft conservative growth recommendations
- Draft store listing copy suggestions
- Suggest experiments
- Summarize review themes
- Highlight what metric to watch next

## What the AI may not do

- Recommend fake reviews
- Recommend fake installs
- Recommend keyword stuffing
- Recommend misleading claims
- Recommend review manipulation
- Recommend changing Play Console data automatically
- Recommend changing Google Ads budgets or campaigns automatically

## Required fields for recommendations

- Title
- Category
- Priority
- Confidence
- Evidence
- Exact suggested manual action
- Risk level
- Why this matters
- Metric to watch after implementation

## Risk levels

- `low`
- `medium`
- `high`

High risk advice is allowed only as advice. It is never executed by software.
